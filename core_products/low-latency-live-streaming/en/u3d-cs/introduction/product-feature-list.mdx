## Audio

### Basic Features

| Basic Features-20% | Feature Description-50% | Use Cases-30% |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Audio Spectrum and Volume Changes](/live-streaming-u3d/audio/sound-level-spectrum) | Audio spectrum: the energy value of digital audio signals at each frequency point. Volume change: the volume of a certain stream. | <ul><li>Determine which user on the microphone is speaking, whether the microphone, speaker, etc. are available.</li><li>Audio spectrum animation display.</li></ul> |
| [Headphone Monitoring and Channel Settings](/live-streaming-u3d/audio/headphone-monitor) | Headphone collection monitoring. After inserting headphones (regular headphones or Bluetooth headphones) on the device, you can hear the sound collected by the device's microphone from the local headphone side. | <ul><li>Show live streaming.</li><li>Emotional FM.</li><li>Music teaching and other more professional scenarios.</li></ul> |
| [Audio 3A Processing](/live-streaming-u3d/audio/audio-3a-processing) | During real-time audio and video calls or live streaming, the audio can be processed with 3A to improve call or live streaming quality and user experience.<ul><li>AEC (Acoustic Echo Cancellation): Filter the collected audio data to reduce echoes in the audio.</li><li>AGC (Automatic Gain Control): After enabling this function, the SDK can automatically adjust the microphone volume to adapt to near and far sound pickup and maintain stable volume.</li><li>ANS (Automatic Noise Suppression): Identify background noise in the sound and eliminate it. After enabling this function, the human voice can be clearer.</li></ul> | All scenarios that hope to have high-quality real-time audio and video services. |
