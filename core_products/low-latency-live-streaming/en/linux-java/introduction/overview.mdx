# Overview

---

Express Video is a real-time audio and video interactive service product that can provide developers with convenient access, highly reliable, and multi-platform interoperable audio and video services. With an end-to-end average latency as low as 200 ms, an industry-leading QoS strategy for weak network quality assurance, and combined with powerful 3A processing capabilities, it perfectly supports one-to-many, many-to-many real-time audio and video calls, live streaming, conferences, and other scenarios.

Express Video also provides developers with the ability to access audio and video services across all platforms with 4 lines of code, enabling developers to build products and services with perfect audio and video experiences within 30 minutes.

For related concepts, please refer to [Glossary](/glossary/term-explanation).

## Product Advantages

#### Intelligent and Realistic Picture Quality

- Deep device adaptation: Deeply adapted to various cameras online, achieving extremely low capture latency and high picture quality.
- Ultra-high picture quality: Supports up to 4K 60fps ultra-high quality video.
- Self-developed encoder: Self-developed optimized codec z264, with higher picture quality under the same conditions, and extremely obvious improvement in picture quality for complex scenes.
- AI video engine: Self-developed AI video engine supports multi-fold super-resolution, low-light enhancement, background segmentation, frame interpolation, and other capabilities, comprehensively improving picture quality.

#### Immersive Sound Quality

- Deep device adaptation: Deeply adapted to various microphones, sound cards, and other devices, achieving low-latency capture, low-latency ear return, and ultra-high fidelity sound quality.
- Rich sound effects: Optimized by professional audio team, there are more than 30 sound effect scenarios such as voice changer, beautification, reverb, and virtual stereo.
- 3A and scenario-based AI noise reduction: Self-developed 3A algorithm and the industry's lightest AI noise reduction achieve pure human voice after noise and echo suppression with extremely low performance loss. Industry-first scenario-based AI noise reduction, real-time scenario recognition, intelligently ensuring the comprehensive effect of noise reduction and sound quality.
- Range audio and video: Combining distance, direction, transmission medium, etc., using professional algorithms to simulate realistic immersive audio and video call experience.

#### Unlimited Gameplay and Scenarios

- Large-scale co-hosting interaction: No limit on the number of people in the room, supporting up to 10,000 people on-mic audio and video calls, helping virtual concerts, 10,000-person meetings, and other scenarios.
- Real-time state synchronization: Supports tens of thousands of people for ordered, high-frequency, and low-latency synchronization of state information such as positions and avatars and custom signaling.
- Security and compliance: Provides solutions such as cloud proxy for firewall environments, and capabilities such as data fencing for data compliance requirements in different regions.
- Cloud services and components: Satisfies more diverse gameplay, providing various services and components such as stream mixing and transcoding, audio review, video review, recording and screenshots.

#### Massive Capability APIs

- Basic audio and video: Provides the ability to implement 1v1 audio and video calls, multi-person audio and video calls, and multi-room audio and video calls.
- Advanced video processing: Provides layered coding, super-resolution, low-light processing, background segmentation, basic beauty, capture rotation, screen sharing, watermarks, and screenshots.
- Advanced audio processing: Provides 3A, scenario-based AI noise reduction, voice changer & beautification & reverb, spatial audio effects, ear return, range audio and video, voice detection, and other capabilities.
- Value-added cloud services: Provides stream mixing and transcoding, CDN relay, low-bitrate HD, 10,000-person RTC co-hosting, ultra-low latency live streaming, 10,000-person real-time state synchronization, media stream input, SIP interoperability, and other capabilities.
- Quality services: Provides pre-call device detection, real-time network detection, in-call quality insights, full-link operation and maintenance (Analytics Dashboard), and other capabilities.
- Multiple ecosystem components: Provides components such as audio review, video review, advanced beauty filters and stickers, Super Board, recording and screenshots, In-app Chat (ZIM).

#### Global High-Availability Service

- Deep adaptation for multiple terminals and platform languages: Deeply adapted to various headphones, sound cards, cameras, and other peripherals, adapted to 15,000+ terminal devices and IoT devices, compatible with 25+ languages and development platforms.
- High availability in complex network environments: Audio resists up to 80% packet loss, video resists 70% packet loss, can achieve 1000ms ultra-strong anti-jitter capability, network bandwidth limitation is as low as 30kbps.
- Global coverage of massive ordered network MSDN: Provides 500+ nodes, covering 212 countries, with stable transmission network. End-to-end latency as low as 79ms, comparable to real-life audio and video experience, long-distance end-to-end transmission latency averages 300ms.
- Up to 99.99% service high availability: Provides high-performance architecture that can accommodate tens of millions of high concurrency. According to online scale, achieves second-level smooth expansion, while providing Analytics Dashboard products for real-time quality tracking, locating and solving problems.
