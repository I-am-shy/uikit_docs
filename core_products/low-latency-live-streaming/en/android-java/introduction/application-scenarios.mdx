# Application scenarios

- - -

### Overseas live streaming

- Tackle pain points such as stuttering, slow first frame, and poor image quality one by one, improving the live streaming experience for global viewers, allowing global users to enjoy live streaming social interaction.
- Break through bottlenecks such as poor overseas network environments, high proportion of mid-to-low-end devices, and poor streaming environments for amateur anchors, helping enterprises with overseas localization.


### Showroom live streaming

- Provides essential showroom live streaming features such as instant opening and AI effects, supporting seamless switching solutions for multiple scenarios including cross-room PK and live streaming to 1-on-1.
- While ensuring the live viewing experience for all viewers, it focuses on guaranteeing the experience quality of top anchors and paid users, effectively promoting paid user growth.


### Large online classes

- Millisecond-level latency enables easy interaction between teachers and students, keeping content strongly synchronized among different students.
- Guarantees the learning quality of paid courses, making online classrooms as smooth and natural as offline teaching.


### Game live streaming

- In high-bitrate game scenarios, adaptive transcoding based on viewer bandwidth effectively reduces stuttering rates, allowing all viewers to watch game interaction smoothly.
- In complex dynamic game scenes, achieves same-bitrate HD low-bitrate coding, effectively improving game image quality without being limited by viewer network bottlenecks.


### Event live streaming

- Ultra-low stuttering: Full-link self-developed private transmission protocol + adaptive quality switching, achieving a stuttering rate of < 1%, missing no highlight moments.
- Ultra-low latency and ultra-strong synchronization: Millisecond-level latency and high synchronization with event progress, grasping arena dynamics in real time.
- Ultra-clear image quality: Supports 4K 60fps and HDR, presenting rich event details and silky ultra-clear dynamic footage.


### E-commerce live streaming

- Matches the strong real-time requirements of selling products, with anchors responding quickly to viewers' shopping inquiries, increasing stickiness between anchors and viewers.
- Achieves strong synchronization and fairness for activities such as auctions, flash sales, and limited-time offers, stimulating live room users' conversion to orders.

### Watch together

- Ultra-strong image synchronization enables communication and interaction with friends based on the same frame without spoilers, sharing exciting moments.
- Seamless smooth switching to RTC, compared to traditional CDN live streaming mic connection, is smoother, achieving co-hosting interaction with < 400ms latency.
