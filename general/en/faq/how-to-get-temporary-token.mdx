# How to get a temporary Token for calling ZEGO SDK?


As shown in the form below, enter the valid ServerSecret, AppID obtained from the [ZEGOCLOUD Console](https://console.zegocloud.com/) and UserID, and validity period (seconds, optional payload) , then click "Generate Token" to get a temporary Token for debugging the ZEGO SDK:

<Warning title="Note">
Temporary Tokens are for testing and verification only. For production environment integration guidance, please refer to: [Using Token Authentication](/real-time-video-ios-oc/communication/using-token-authentication).
</Warning>
